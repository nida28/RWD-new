Hotels In Mauritius

In the past, accommodation meant staying on the exclusive east coast. Now, with around 120 hotels spread across the island, there's a five star establishment at every turn, with butlers, private plunge pools and helicopter transfers, and increasingly, a selection of decent four stars. Mauritius has eight top-flight golf courses, all attached to hotels, world-class spas offering beauty, wellbeing and fitness programmes, and gourmet restaurants.
